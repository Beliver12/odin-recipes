Eggplant Parmesan Recipes.

Description.

Think of Eggplant Parmigiana as a pasta-less vegetarian Lasagna, made with thin slices of eggplant instead of pasta, layered with tomato sauce, fresh basil and plenty of molten cheese. Called Parmigiana di Melanzane in Italian and known as Eggplant Parmesan in the US, it’s a low-carb dream come true!

10 Ingredients

  • 3 (about 350g each) eggplant, cut into 1cm-thick slices
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small brown onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 700g bottle passata
  • 500g tub smooth riccota
  • 70g(1cup) finely grated parmesan
  • 6 fresh lasagne sheets
  • 50g (1/2 cup) coarsely grated mozzarella
  • Fresh oregano, to serve

"Steps"

  1. Step-1

    Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan forced. Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Spray the eggplant slices with oil spray. Working in batches, cook the eggplant for 3-4 minutes each side or until soft and golden brown. Transfer to a plate.
